My name is Mustafa Muhammad from Philadelphia, PA. I was raised in West Philadelphia, and eventually moved to the Mt. Airy sectin of Philadelphia. I soon went on to catholic school attending Roman Catholic High (downtown Philadelphia), and transferred to, and graduated from Bishop McDevitt High

Most of my career has been in retail as I grew up learning the ins and outs of the retail world through my mothers store that she had for over 15 years! I currently attent the University of Pennsylvania's College of Liberal and Professional Studies Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp program. My goals are to eventually become a full fledge web developer at a respective company.
